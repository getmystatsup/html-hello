Visual stories built with pigment, texture, and memory.

I am Aria Vale, a visual artist based in Lisbon. My work explores transitions between natural terrains and emotional landscapes through mixed media, large-scale canvas, and digital composition.

About Me

My practice combines acrylic, charcoal, and found materials to create tactile works that invite close reading. Each piece starts with field sketches and sound recordings collected while traveling coastal regions.
